About me

Hi, I am a professional architectural draughtsperson with over 8 years of experience in the industry. I have worked on a variety of projects, from small homes to large commercial buildings. I have a detailed understanding of national and local building regulations, and I am proficient in using CAD software to create high-quality drawings. I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented individual with a strong work ethic. I am able to work independently or as part of a team, and I am always willing to go the extra mile to get the job done. I am also a quick learner and I am always eager to take on new challenges. In my current role, I am responsible for developing all of the drawings for the construction and marketing of structures. I also work closely with clients and contractors to ensure that the drawings are accurate and meet all applicable regulations. I am organized, efficient, and I meet deadlines consistently. I am also a team player and I am able to work effectively with others to achieve common goals. I am passionate about architecture and I am always learning new things. I am eager to apply my skills to new projects and I am confident that I have the skills and experience necessary to be successful in this role.
